Tips for Finding an Email Address

  1. Look at the header and footer of the article for a links to email the author or to the author's profile.
     
  2. Search Google for the author's personal website
     
  3. Try the following searches on Google:
    1. “[first name]@[website they write for].com” ex: "john@lifehacker.com"
    2. “[first initial][last name]@[domain].com” ex: "jsmith@lifehacker.com"
    3. “[first name].[last name]@[domain].com” ex: "john.smith@lifehacker.com"
       
  4. Try this tool: http://linksy.me/find-email (but please make sure the match it suggests make sense)
     
  5. Use Rapportive (free) as described here: http://www.distilled.net/blog/miscellaneous/find-almost-anybodys-email-address/
